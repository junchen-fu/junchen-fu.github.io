About Me

I am a first-year PhD student advised by Prof. Joemon Jose at University of Glasgow. Before this, I was a Research Assistant at Westlake University, focusing on Modality-based Recommender Systems. My current research interests are in:

  • Efficiency in multimodal recommendation and information retrieval systems
  • Multimodal Generative Models
