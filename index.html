About Me

I am a first-year PhD student advised by Prof. Joemon Jose at University of Glasgow. Before this, I was a Research Assistant at Westlake University, advised by Prof. Fajie Yuan. My research interest is in building efficient multimodal recommendation and information retrieval systems.
