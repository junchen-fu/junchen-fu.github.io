About Me

I am a first-year PhD student advised by Prof. Joemon Jose at University of Glasgow. Before this, I was a Research Assistant at Westlake University, advised by Prof. Fajie Yuan. My recent research interest is in the efficiency for recommender systems (RS), information retrieval (IR), computer vision (CV), natural language processing (NLP) and multimodality tasks. If you are interested in the collaboration, feel free to drop me an email.
